Empower your community

Start a Plates to Purpose chapter and help fight food waste and hunger where you live. It’s easy to start a chapter — here’s how, in six steps.

The Process

Start a Plates to Purpose chapter

1

Connect with us

Reach out to platestopurpose@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram at @platestopurpose. Our central team will help answer questions, share resources, and guide you through the chapter process.

2

Build your team

Gather a dedicated group of volunteers who are passionate about reducing food waste and fighting hunger in your community.

3

Partner with receiving organizations

Connect with at least two local food banks, shelters, pantries, or community organizations that can safely receive and distribute donated food.

4

Find local food donors

Reach out to restaurants, bakeries, donut shops, school cafeterias, grocery stores, and local businesses to build food donation partnerships. Start small, then work toward consistent weekly or biweekly pickups.

5

Track and share your impact

Record every donation, including the food source, amount donated, receiving organization, and photos when possible. This helps your chapter stay organized and show the impact you are making.

6

Grow your chapter

Once your chapter has reliable donors and volunteers, begin expanding to school districts, city leaders, local businesses, grants, and larger community partnerships.
