Published Research · IJRISS · October 2025

The Dual Crisis of Food Wastage and Food Insecurity in the Dallas-Fort Worth Metropolitan Area

Peer-reviewed research by our founders, Parin Chanana and Mihir Kakarala, published in the International Journal of Research and Innovation in Social Science. The study maps the scale of food waste and food insecurity across DFW — where 405,000 tons of food residuals are landfilled annually while 1 in 7 residents faces food insecurity — and proposes policy, infrastructure, and community solutions.
