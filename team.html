Meet the National Executive Team

Plates to Purpose is led entirely by students who believe age is no barrier to making a lasting impact in the community.

Mihir founded Plates to Purpose after learning about the realities of food insecurity and food waste so close to home, inspiring him to make a hands-on impact in his community. Today, Mihir leads the organization’s strategy, partnerships, and operations to rescue surplus food and deliver it to families and individuals in need.

Mihir Kakarala

Parin oversees day-to-day operations, translating the organization’s broader strategy into coordinated food recovery pickups, volunteer efforts, and donation logistics. Beyond daily execution, he leads expansion and outreach by creating partnerships, securing funding through grants, streamlining operational systems, and establishing chapters across locations to maximize the organization’s community impact.
